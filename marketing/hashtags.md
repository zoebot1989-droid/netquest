## Hashtag Usage Rules

1. **TikTok:** 5-8 hashtags. Always include `#fyp` and `#techtok`. Mix niche + broad.
2. **Twitter/X:** 2-3 max. Integrate naturally into the tweet when possible.
3. **Instagram:** 20-30 in first comment (not caption). Mix sizes (big + niche).
4. **Reddit:** No hashtags. They don't work and look cringe.
5. **Always include `#netquest`** on TikTok and Instagram for brand tracking.
